# Sales Funnel for Muslim Sales Coach

## Project Overview
- **Project Name**: The Ultimate Provider Brotherhood
- **Type**: Lead capture funnel with video sales page
- **Core Functionality**: Convert Muslim professionals stuck in low-paying jobs into remote sales career clients
- **Target Users**: Muslims earning 35-50k/year seeking to earn 5-12k+/month in remote sales

## Visual & Rendering Specification

### Tech Stack
- Next.js 14 (App Router)
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- shadcn/ui (components)
- Magic UI (beautiful effects: particles, blur, gradients)
- Acertinity UI (animations)

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep emerald green (#059669) - represents growth, prosperity in Islamic culture
- **Secondary**: Rich gold (#D4A853) - represents success, wealth
- **Background**: Off-white (#FAF9F6) with subtle warm undertones
- **Text**: Dark charcoal (#1F2937)
- **Accent**: Soft cream (#FEF3E2)

### Typography
- **Headings**: Playfair Display (elegant, authoritative)
- **Body**: DM Sans (modern, clean, highly readable)

### Visual Effects
- Smooth scroll animations
- Gradient overlays on sections
- Floating particle effects (subtle, gold/green)
- Glassmorphism cards
- Elegant dividers with Islamic geometric patterns

## Page Structure

### 1. Hero Section
- Full-screen video background (YouTube embed with custom poster)
- Overlay with headline and CTA
- Headline: "Turn Your Sales Skills Into a $5K-$12K/Month Remote Income"
- Subheadline targeting the pain point
- CTA button: "Start Your Journey"

### 2. Social Proof Section
- Instagram embed/follow button
- Stats: 15 Years Experience, 100+ Reps Coached
- "The Ultimate Provider Brotherhood" branding

### 3. Problem/Solution Section
- Clear value proposition
- Why remote sales, why now
- Benefits for Muslim professionals

### 4. About The Coach
- Brief bio emphasizing experience
- Trust signals

### 5. Testimonials (placeholder)
- Can be added later

### 6. CTA Section
- Final call to action
- Contact form/booking

### 7. Footer
- Instagram link, basic links

## Interaction Specification
- Smooth scroll to sections
- Animated entrance on scroll
- Video autoplay disabled (user interaction required)
- All buttons have hover states
- Mobile responsive (critical for DM-based business)
